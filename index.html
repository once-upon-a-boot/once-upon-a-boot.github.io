Once upon a boot

Kernels, firmwares and bootloaders are complex to understand.
This website gives you a unique opportunity to start exploring them, by showing what is happening when something is printed on the console.

Based on QEMU with uftrace plugin, uftrace and perfetto to visualize traces.
This website and traces are generated using uftrace-web-viewer from qemu-linux-stack.

Choose your story

Tutorial: Expand a track by clicking on the left of screen. Zoom and scroll traces using W,A,S,D keys. Click on a function to see its source code.
